Xuehan Xiong

I am carrying out a PhD at the Robotics Institute (Carnegie Mellon University) under the supervision of Fernando De la Torre. My research focuses on Computer Vision, in particular, nonrigid object detection and tracking. During my Masters, I was working with Daniel Huber on context-based recognition of building components.

Before arriving at CMU, I was studying Computer Science at the University of Arizona and also ran track for the Wildcats. In 2007, I received the All-American honor thanks to my teammates, Marcus, Bobby, Troy, and Antoine and of course Coach Harvey.

I was born in Wuhan, China and raised by two great parents who are professional track and field coaches. Under their Influence, I started my track and field career at a young age. In 2004, I won the national junior championship in Men's 200m dash and broke the national junior record in the same event.
